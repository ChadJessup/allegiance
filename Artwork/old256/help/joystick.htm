Joystick Controls

 
Button1 Fires weapon
Button 2 Afterburner
Button 3 Fires Missile
Button 4 Switches weapon
Button 5 Locks vector
Hat Switches between left, up, down, and right views
Throttle Adjusts speed
Forward on the stick Nose down
Back on the stick Nose up
Joystick left Turn left
Joystick right Turn right
Twist joystick handle Rotate ship
