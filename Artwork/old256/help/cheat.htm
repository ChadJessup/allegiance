Cheat Codes

 

CreateDrones [DroneType] [[# of drones] [DroneSide] ["default command"] [shipTypeID]]

Notice that drone type is the only mandatory parameter.

DroneType = miner, turret, or wingman

# of drones = pos. int

drone side = the number of your side (Iron League = 0, Full House = 1, Rix = 2)

 

Stations

Lists all the stations, who owns them, their hp, and where they are

 

Sides

Lists all the sides, how many stations they have, how many players they have, the names of each player, the ship they're flying, whether they're docked (and what station), and their position in xyz and sector.

 

Capture #

Captures a station. Specify by number shown in Stations list
